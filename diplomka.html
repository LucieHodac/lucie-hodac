Something different
that it may seem

This project focuses on the human need to preserve confidentiality and the associated ownership of inaccessible places. The outcome is a collection of porcelain boxes in different forms with specific features and thanks to them they can be called “secret”. A set of boxes consists of two jars with unique latching system - a bottomless vase and a statue of the guardian.
