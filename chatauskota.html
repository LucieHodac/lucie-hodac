Chata U Skota

Chata U Skota is a family guesthouse located in Hrabětice in the Jizera Mountains. It is situated in a beautiful and peaceful environment, making it an excellent destination for both winter and summer family vacations. I had the opportunity to get to know the place thoroughly and then designed a logo and elements of the visual identity for the owners, including Instagram templates. I also helped with small decorative features used in the guesthouse and created original drawings of locations on islands after which the individual rooms are named.
